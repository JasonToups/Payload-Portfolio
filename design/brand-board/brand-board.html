07 · Direction
Do

Amber as atmosphere, not accent.

Warm amber carries 30–60% of any given screen. It is the atmosphere, not a button highlight on an otherwise gray page.

Don't

Never use pure white or pure black.

Every surface carries the amber warmth tint. No surface is ever #ffffff or #000000 — chroma 0.005–0.01 in OKLCH is enough.

Do

Subscribe before scroll.

The newsletter is the primary conversion. The subscribe CTA belongs in the hero and at least one mid-page section — never buried in the footer.

Don't

No gradient text or side-stripe borders.

background-clip: text is decorative, never meaningful. Side-stripe borders above 1px on cards or callouts are banned. Use background tints or nothing.

Do

Fraunces at Black weight only.

Fraunces appears at Display, Headline, and Title levels only. Never body copy or labels. Its authority comes from rarity and weight — light cuts collapse the contrast.

Don't

No motion that can't be turned off.

Every entrance animation and scroll sequence must be gated by prefers-reduced-motion. State transitions (color, opacity) survive; translate/scale animations are suppressed.
